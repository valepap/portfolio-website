Virtuality

For this app, I reworked on the opening credits and environments of Brett Leonard's film Virtuality, through a personal interpretation, to restore the film's atmosphere and key points. The opening credits were designed in the Soul Bass style, characterised by essential graphics.

The virtual model allows you to an immersive and exploratory experience in two levels: the prison and the street and the TV studio, two crucial settings in the film.

Furthermore, we introduced other important elements, such as the glass, the hologram of the virtual entity and other effects to emphasise the concept of virtual reality, such as wireframe buildings and fading street lights.

Design:

  • 3D modeling
  • Scene composition: materials, textures, lighting, animations
  • Interactions with Visual Scripting (Unreal Blueprint).
